Role-based permissions

Each principal is assigned a role. The memory adapter resolves role-derived grants; a second source provides a principal-specific denial. Mizan evaluates all facts and makes the final decision.

Schedule awareness

A permission can be scoped to a recurring schedule (e.g., business hours only). Mizan evaluates the schedule against the evaluation timestamp and returns outside-schedule when the current time falls outside the window.

How it works: Adapters (like MemoryAdapter) provide authorization facts — role definitions, assignments, and principal-specific rules. The @mizan/core engine collects those facts, evaluates them against the requested permission, scope, temporal window, and schedule, then returns a final allow or deny with a stable reason code. Adapters provide facts; Mizan makes the final decision.
